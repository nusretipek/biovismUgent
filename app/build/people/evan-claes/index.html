Evan Claes
Ph.D. Student
Ghent University (UGent)
Evan Claes is a PhD student in Bioscience Engineering with a strong background in bio-science, data analysis and hybrid modelling. He is affiliated with Antleron NV. His current research focus on analytical Quality-by-Design and hybrid modelling to enable technologies for ATMP manufacturing 4.0.
