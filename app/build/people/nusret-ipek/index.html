Nusret Ipek
Ph.D. Student
Ghent University (UGent)
Nusret Ipek is a PhD student in Bioscience Engineering with a strong background in statistics, data analysis and computer programming. His current research involves automated analysis of caged animal videos and developing algorithms to deduct behavioral knowledge using machine learning.
